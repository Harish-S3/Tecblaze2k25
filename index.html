About
SRM Institute of Science and Technology, Ramapuram (SRM IST Ramapuram) is a premier educational institution known for its academic excellence, state-of-the-art research, and industry-focused curriculum. Located in the heart of Chennai, SRM IST Ramapuram fosters a culture of innovation, interdisciplinary learning, and entrepreneurship.
TecBlaze 2K25 is a prestigious national-level technical symposium organized annually by the Department of Computer Science and Applications. This dynamic event serves as a platform for students to showcase their technical expertise, creativity, and problem-solving abilities through a series of engaging competitions, workshops, and interactive sessions.
Offline Events
Paper Presentation

Thesis Thunder

Thesis Thunder is a research paper presentation event at TecBlaze Symposium. This is your opportunity to showcase groundbreaking research in AI, Data Science, Cybersecurity, IoT, Blockchain, and more! Present your work to a panel of experts and gain valuable insights into academic and industry research.

Project Contest

Project Contest

The Project Contest is a technical event where participants showcase innovative projects that solve real-world problems using technology. Participants must develop their projects beforehand and present them to a panel of judges. The contest encourages creativity, problem-solving, and technical excellence.

Web Design

Web Wizards

Web Wizards is a web design competition at TecBlaze Symposium. Unleash your creativity and develop a unique, interactive, and responsive webpage within the given time frame. Compete with fellow developers, showcase your skills, and impress the judges with your innovative designs.

Poster Making

Infographics

Infographics is a Poster/Logo Making Competition at TecBlaze Symposium. Express your creativity and design a visually compelling poster / Logo that conveys a strong message and bring your ideas to life using digital tools!

Coding Competition

Codezen

CodeZen is an intense coding competition designed to challenge problem-solving skills across multiple programming languages. Participants will compete in a series of rounds to test their logic, debugging, and coding efficiency.

Guess the Word

Connix

Connections is a fast-paced puzzle-solving challenge where teams decode visual clues and make quick connections. Race against time to prove your problem-solving skills and emerge victorious!

Quiz

Quizzera

Quizzera is the ultimate general knowledge battle at TecBlaze! Compete in a high-energy quiz event where teams tackle multiple rounds of brain-teasing questions to prove their knowledge and claim victory!

IPL Auction

IPL Auction

The IPL Auction event tests participants' strategic thinking, budgeting skills, and knowledge of cricket players. Teams will participate in a bidding war to create the most balanced and efficient squad under a fixed budget.

Online Events
Photography Contest

Digital Imaging

The Digital Imaging challenges participants to capture stunning images/Clips based on a given theme. Whether using a Camera or phone camera, showcase your creativity and storytelling skills through photography!

BGMI

Game Engine

BGMI & FF Tournament is an intense online gaming competition where teams battle it out to claim victory. Strategy, teamwork, and skill will determine the ultimate champions.
