Project Contest
From Concept to Creation.
Description
The Project Contest is a technical event where participants showcase innovative projects that solve real-world problems using technology. Participants must develop their projects beforehand and present them to a panel of judges. The contest encourages creativity, problem-solving, and technical excellence.
|
Important Details
  • 📌 Event Date: 20th March 2025
  • 📌 Time: 11:00 AM – 01:00 PM
  • 📌 Venue: HI-tech Hall-2
  • 📌 Team Size: 1 to 4 members
  • 📌 Project Type: Software, Hardware, or both
Rules & Regulations
  • ✔️ Eligibility: Open to all college students.
  • ✔️ Projects must be original and developed beforehand.
  • ✔️ No plagiarism – Any copied project will lead to disqualification.
  • ✔️ Presentation Time: 5 minutes (with 5 min demo through PPT & 2 min Q&A).
  • ✔️ Participants must bring their own laptops & devices.
  • ✔️ The project must be explained clearly with its features, challenges, and real-world impact.
  • ✔️ Judges’ decisions are final & binding.
